# Legacy DMOs

Data model objects (DMOs) organize and harmonize your data in Data 360. They’re categorized into standard (std\_\_ prefix) and legacy (ssot\_\_ prefix) types. Standard DMOs are flexible, work consistently across all your environments, and receive regular field updates to expand your functional capabilities. For more information, see the [Standard DMOs](https://developer.salesforce.com/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-si-entity-interface-dmos-introduction.html).
