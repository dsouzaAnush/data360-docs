# Standard Data Model Objects (DMOs)

As of October 14, 2025, Data Cloud has been rebranded to Data 360. During this transition, you may see references to Data Cloud in our application and documentation. While the name is new, the functionality and content remains unchanged.

Data model objects (DMOs) are groupings of data created from data streams, insights, and other sources. Find reference information about the standard DMOs found in Data 360.

-   [**Account DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-account-dmo.html)

    The Account DMO represents how a party wants to interact with your company.

-   [**Account Contact DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-account-contact-dmo.html)

    The Account Contact DMO represents an individual who has a role specific to an account.

-   [**Affiliation DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-affiliation-dmo.html)

    The Affiliation DMO represents affiliation or how to map Marketing Cloud Engagement business unit use case data.

-   [**Agent Service Presence DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-agent-service-presence-dmo.html)

    The Agent Service Presence DMO represents a user's real-time presence status.

-   [**Agent Work DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-agent-work-dmo.html)

    The Agent Work DMO represents a work assignment that's been routed to an agent.

-   [**Agent Work Skill DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-agent-work-skill-dmo.html)

    The Agent Work Skill DMO represents a skill that's used to route a work assignment to an agent.

-   [**Applicant DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-applicant-dmo.html)

    The Applicant DMO represents an individual or group that's requesting something from an organization, such as a loan or admission to a university.

-   [**Asset Service Level Objective DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-asset-service-level-objective-dmo.html)

    The Asset Service Level Objective (SLO) DMO represents SLO data that's transmitted over a network, such as a computer network or a telecommunications network. SLO data stores details, such as source and destination IP addresses, timestamps, and data types (for example, TCP and UDP).

-   [**Asset Service Level Objective Consequence DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-asset-service-level-objective-consequence-dmo.html)

    The Asset Service Level Objective (SLO) Consequence DMO represents network communications data that's sent over a network when the SLO isn't met. These are computer networks or telecommunications networks that include all data packets sent and received over a network, such as emails, voice calls, video streaming, web browsing, or any other type of data transfer.

-   [**Authorization Form DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-authorization-form-dmo.html)

    The Authorization Form DMO represents a set of terms and conditions, such as a privacy policy, a contract, or consent forms.

-   [**Authorization Form Consent DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-authorization-form-consent-dmo.html)

    The Authorization Form Consent DMO represents capturing where, when, and how a party gives consent for a form, a set of terms and conditions, or a privacy policy.

-   [**Authorization Form Data Use DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-authorization-form-data-use-dmo.html)

    The Authorization Form Data Use DMO represents the data uses consented to in an authorization form.

-   [**Authorization Form Text DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-authorization-form-text-dmo.html)

    The Authorization Form Text DMO represents an authorization form's text and language settings.

-   [**Benefit Action DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-benefit-action-dmo.html)

    The Benefit Action DMO represents actions triggered when a program benefit is assigned to a loyalty program member.

-   [**Benefit Type DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-benefit-type-dmo.html)

    The Benefit Type DMO represents the types of benefits of a loyalty program, such as healthcare, financial, and loyalty.

-   [**Brand DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-brand-dmo.html)

    The Brand DMO represents the product's brand, such as Northern Trail Outfitters.

-   [**Card Account DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-card-account-dmo.html)

    The Card Account DMO represents a financial tool offered by a bank as a type of loan, with a line of revolving credit that you can access via your card and your card's account.

-   [**Case DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-case.html)

    The Case DMO represents support cases based on recorded issues, such as a laptop connectivity problem.

-   [**Case Update DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-case-update-dmo.html)

    The Case Update DMO represents historical information about changes made to the associated case.

-   [**Communication Subscription DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-communication-subscription-dmo.html)

    The Communication Subscription DMO represents a customer's subscription preferences for a specific communication.

-   [**Communication Subscription Channel Type DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-communication-subscription-channel-type-dmo.html)

    The Communication Subscription Channel Type DMO represents the engagement channel where a customer can be contacted for a communication subscription.

-   [**Communication Subscription Consent DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-communication-subscription-consent-dmo.html)

    The Communication Subscription Consent DMO represents a customer's engagement or communication channel preferences.

-   [**Communication Subscription Timing DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-communication-subscription-timing-dmo.html)

    The Communication Subscription Timing DMO represents a customer's timing preferences for receiving a communication subscription.

-   [**Consent Action DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-consent-action-dmo.html)

    The Consent Action DMO represents what a user consents to regarding their data, such as data collection or web activity tracking.

-   [**Consent Status DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-consent-status-dmo.html)

    The Consent Status DMO represents the status of consent, such as opted in or opted out of data collection.

-   [**Contact Point Address DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-contact-point-address-dmo.html)

    The Contact Point Address DMO represents the mailing address of a party.

-   [**Contact Point App DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-contact-point-app-dmo.html)

    The Contact Point App DMO represents the software application of a party on a specific device.

-   [**Contact Point Consent DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-contact-point-consent-dmo.html)

    The Contact Point Consent DMO represents the consent details for a specific contact point, including the date, method, duration, and whether the party has double opted-in.

-   [**Contact Point Digital ID DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-contact-point-digitalId-dmo.html)

    The Contact Point Digital ID DMO represents enabling customers to ingest, harmonize, and activate various types of digital identifiers along with their values. This object supports known and anonymous first-party identifiers.

-   [**Contact Point Email DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-contact-point-email-dmo.html)

    The Contact Point Email DMO represents the email address of a party.

-   [**Contact Point Phone DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-contact-point-phone-dmo.html)

    The Contact Point Phone DMO represents the phone number of a party.

-   [**Contact Point Social DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-contact-point-social-dmo.html)

    The Contact Point Social DMO represents the social media handle for a party, such as @trustednews on Twitter.

-   [**Conversation Entry Transcript Excerpt DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-conversation-entry-transcript-excerpt-dmo.html)

    The Conversation Entry Transcript Excerpt DMO represents a portion of a transcript from a conversation entry, for example, "I need to cancel my order."

-   [**Conversation Reason DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-conversation-reason-dmo.html)

    The Conversation Reason DMO represents the reason a conversation started. It contains aggregated metrics for excerpts, such as cancel order, update order, and check on order status.

-   [**Conversation Reason Category DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-conversation-reason-category-dmo.html)

    The Conversation Reason Category DMO represents a grouping of conversation reasons that have the same overall topic. It contains aggregated metrics for the associated conversation reasons, such as order management, payments, and account management.

-   [**Conversation Reason Report Definition DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-conversation-reason-report-definition-dmo.html)

    The Conversation Reason Report Definition DMO represents an overview of the conversational data structure, including groups of conversation reasons and excerpts.

-   [**Conversation Reason Report Segment Def DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-conversation-reason-report-segment-def-dmo.html)

    The Conversation Reason Report Segment Def DMO represents a segment definition of a conversation reason report.

-   [**Data Use Legal Basis DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-data-use-legal-basis-dmo.html)

    The Data Use Legal Basis DMO represents the legal reason for contacting a customer, such as billing or a contract.

-   [**Data Use Purpose DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-data-use-purpose-dmo.html)

    The Data Use Purpose DMO represents the purpose of contacting a prospect or customer, such as billing, marketing, or surveys.

-   [**Data Use Purpose Consent Action DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-data-use-purpose-consent-action-dmo.html)

    The Data Use Purpose Consent Action DMO represents an individual's consent preferences for consent actions.

-   [**Deposit Account DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-deposit-account-dmo.html)

    The Deposit Account DMO represents a subtype of a financial account, such as a savings account.

-   [**Device DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-device-dmo.html)

    The Device DMO represents a specific electronic unit you want to track signals from, such as a refrigerator, watch, or car.

-   [**Device Application Engagement DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-device-application-engagement-dmo.html)

    The Device Application Engagement DMO represents data about device engagement, such as mobile app usage.

-   [**Device Application Template DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-device-application-template-dmo.html)

    The Device Application Template DMO represents a reusable standard format for applications to exchange information between devices.

-   [**Email Engagement DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-email-engagement-dmo.html)

    The Email Engagement DMO represents data captured from various sources about engagement in the email channel.

-   [**Email Message DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-email-message-dmo.html)

    The Email Message DMO represents an email message, which can be in SMS or HTML format, and attachments sent or received over the network.

-   [**Email Publication DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-email-publication-dmo.html)

    The Email Publication DMO represents a publication, such as a campaign or an orchestration, used in the email channel.

-   [**Email Template DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-email-template-dmo.html)

    The Email Template DMO represents the standard form of an email message that can be personalized and customized based on a campaign.

-   [**Engagement Analysis Text DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-engagement-analysis-text-dmo.html)

    The Engagement Analysis Text DMO represents a part of an engagement session being analyzed, such as a set of chat messages between an agent and customer.

-   [**Engagement Analysis Text Direct Feedback DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-engagement-analysis-text-direct-feedback-dmo.html)

    The Engagement Analysis Text Direct Feedback DMO represents the feedback on a part of an engagement session being analyzed. For example, a user can give a numeric rating of the engagement in a survey.

-   [**Engagement Analysis Text Participant DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-engagement-analysis-text-participant-dmo.html)

    The Engagement Analysis Text Participant DMO represents the participants involved in an engagement session. For example, an agent and a customer are participants in a chat.

-   [**Engagement Analysis Text Session DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-engagement-analysis-text-session-dmo.html)

    The Engagement Analysis Text Session DMO represents an engagement session during a particular time period with multiple forms. For example, a chat session, a survey response, and a case can all occur during one customer's session.

-   [**Engagement Channel Type DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-engagement-channel-type-dmo.html)

    The Engagement Channel Type DMO represents the channels supported by individual preferences. For example, individuals can set consent preferences for SMS but not for a phone call.

-   [**Engagement Channel Type Consent DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-engagement-channel-type-consent-dmo.html)

    The Engagement Channel Type Consent DMO represents an individual's consent preferences specific to a type of communication, such as email.

-   [**Engagement Topic DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-engagement-topic-dmo.html)

    The Engagement Topic DMO represents the topics discussed by a customer and other attendees during an engagement. An engagement can have multiple topics, such as campaigns, promotions, opportunities, and tasks.

-   [**Financial Account DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-account-dmo.html)

    The Financial Account DMO represents a financial account held at a financial institution, such as a bank.

-   [**Financial Account Balance DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-account-balance-dmo.html)

    The Financial Account Balance DMO represents the types of balances associated with a financial account.

-   [**Financial Account Fee DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-account-fee-dmo.html)

    The Financial Account Fee DMO represents fees associated with a financial account.

-   [**Financial Account Interest Rate DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-account-interest-rate-dmo.html)

    The Financial Account Interest Rate DMO represents the interest rate associated with a financial account.

-   [**Financial Account Limit DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-account-limit-dmo.html)

    The Financial Account Limit DMO represents limits associated with a financial account.

-   [**Financial Account Party DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-account-party-dmo.html)

    The Financial Account Party DMO represents the role of an organization account or person account related to a financial account.

-   [**Financial Account Transaction DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-account-transaction-dmo.html)

    The Financial Account Transaction DMO represents the transactions related to a financial account, such as credit or debit.

-   [**Financial Application DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-application-dmo.html)

    The Financial Application DMO represents standard data entry structure used to request something for financial purposes, such as a loan.

-   [**Financial Application Item DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-application-item-dmo.html)

    The Financial Application Item DMO represents an item in an application and its related financial product and attributes.

-   [**Financial Application Item Proposal DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-application-item-proposal-dmo.html)

    The Financial Application Item Proposal DMO represents the terms attached to the scheme to sanction a loan.

-   [**Financial Customer DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-customer-dmo.html)

    The Financial Customer DMO represents an extension of an account to capture financial services attributes.

-   [**Financial Goal DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-goal-dmo.html)

    The Financial Goal DMO represents an amount of money needed to achieve a financial goal, such as education or a home purchase.

-   [**Financial Goal Funding DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-goal-funding-dmo.html)

    The Financial Goal Funding DMO represents a financial goal of an individual or person account that requires funding.

-   [**Financial Goal Party DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-goal-party-dmo.html)

    The Financial Goal Party DMO represents an association between a financial goal with a related party.

-   [**Financial Holding DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-holding-dmo.html)

    The Financial Holding DMO represents the financial holdings associated with an account or a financial account.

-   [**Financial Plan DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-plan-dmo.html)

    The Financial Plan DMO represents a financial plan for a person account.

-   [**Financial Security DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-financial-security-dmo.html)

    The Financial Security DMO represents a financial holding in relation to an account or a financial account, such as securities, bonds, or mutual funds.

-   [**Flow DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-flow-dmo.html)

    The Flow DMO represents the details about a flow business process.

-   [**Flow Element DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-flow-element-dmo.html)

    The Flow Element DMO represents the details about a single element within a flow version.

-   [**Flow Element Run DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-flow-element-run-dmo.html)

    The Flow Element Run DMO represents the status of a single element executed within a flow run.

-   [**Flow Run DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-flow-run-dmo.html)

    The Flow Run DMO represents the details about a single execution of a flow.

-   [**Flow Version DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-flow-version-dmo.html)

    The Flow Version DMO represents the details about a version of a flow.

-   [**Flow Version Occurrence DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-flow-version-occurrence-dmo.html)

    The Flow Version Occurrence DMO represents an instance of a recurring flow that runs on a schedule. For example, a flow runs weekly on Wednesdays and creates an occurrence each time it runs.

-   [**Goods Product DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-goods-product-dmo.html)

    The Goods Product DMO represents a specific product, such as a carton of milk or a set of towels.

-   [**Individual DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-individual-dmo.html)

    The Individual DMO represents contacts, customers, or other people interested in your company's products or services.

-   [**Insurance Policy DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-insurance-policy-dmo.html)

    The Insurance Policy DMO represents an insurance policy, such as home or life.

-   [**Interest Tag Definition DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-interest-tag-definition-dmo.html)

    The Interest Tag Definition DMO represents products, services, and features that a party has expressed interest in.

-   [**Investment Account DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-investment-account-dmo.html)

    The Investment Account DMO represents a type of a financial account, such as a brokerage, an investment, or retirement accounts.

-   [**Knowledge Article Engagement DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-knowledge-article-engagement-dmo.html)

    The Knowledge Article Engagement DMO represents user engagement details related to a knowledge article.

-   [**Lead DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-lead-dmo.html)

    The Lead DMO represents a person or company that shows interest in another company's products or services.

-   [**Loan Account DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loan-account-dmo.html)

    The Loan Account DMO represents a type of a financial account that's used for loans.

-   [**Loyalty Benefit DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-benefit-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Benefit DMO represents a perk or enhacement that's available to members of a loyalty program, such as waived airline baggage fees, complimentary hotel stays, or a rental car upgrade.

-   [**Loyalty Benefit Type DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-benefit-type-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Benefit Type DMO represents the type of loyalty benefit, such as rewards or gift cards.

-   [**Loyalty Journal Subtype DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-journal-subtype-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Journal Subtype DMO represents a subtype of a loyalty journal type, such as a watched video or product review.

-   [**Loyalty Journal Type DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-journal-type-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Journal Type DMO represents the type of loyalty journal, such as accrual, redemption, signup, or expiration.

-   [**Loyalty Ledger DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-ledger-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Ledger DMO represents the points credited or debited for a loyalty program member across various transactions.

-   [**Loyalty Member Currency DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-member-currency-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Member Currency DMO represents the value a loyalty member selects to receive, such as airline miles or points.

-   [**Loyalty Member Tier DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-member-tier-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Member Tier DMO represents the benefit tier within the program that a member is assigned to.

-   [**Loyalty Partner Product DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-partner-product-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Partner Product DMO represents a product offered by a loyalty program partner, such as a coupon from another company.

-   [**Loyalty Program DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-program-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Program DMO represents a strategy designed to encourage customers to remain loyal to the business associated with the program.

-   [**Loyalty Program Currency DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-program-currency-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Program Currency DMO represents the value or currency that the loyalty program offers to customers.

-   [**Loyalty Program Member DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-program-member-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Program Member DMO represents a person who has joined a loyalty program.

-   [**Loyalty Program Member Promotion DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-program-member-promotion-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Program Member Promotion DMO represents a promotion that's available to a loyalty program member. For example, a loyalty program allows double points on outdoor furniture purchases.

-   [**Loyalty Program Partner DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-program-partner-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Program Partner DMO represents companies with loyalty program offerings.

-   [**Loyalty Tier DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-tier-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Tier DMO represents a level in a loyalty program, where members receive better benefits as they move up the hierarchy.

-   [**Loyalty Tier Benefit DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-tier-benefit-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Tier Benefit DMO represents a benefit that's available in a specific loyalty member tier.

-   [**Loyalty Tier Group DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-tier-group-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Tier Group DMO represents loyalty programs that have multiple tiers of benefits. Tiers can be organized based on objectives, such as lifetime, marketing, or regular.

-   [**Loyalty Transaction Journal DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-loyalty-transaction-journal-dmo.html)

    The Loyalty Transaction Journal DMO represents a collection of transactions related to a loyalty program.

-   [**Market Journey Activity DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-market-journey-activity-dmo.html)

    The Market Journey Activity DMO represents a step or activity within a journey in Salesforce Journey Builder.

-   [**Market Segment DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-market-segment-dmo.html)

    The Market Segment DMO represents the grouping of people who share one or more common characteristics for marketing purposes.

-   [**Master Product DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-base-product-dmo.html)

    The Master Product DMO represents data about a company's products.

-   [**Member Benefit DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-member-benefit-dmo.html)

    The Member Benefit DMO represents a benefit that's available within a loyalty program that a member qualifies for and has elected to receive.

-   [**Message Engagement DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-message-engagement-dmo.html)

    The Message Engagement DMO represents a user's engagement with a marketing message.

-   [**Network Usage DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-network-usage-dmo.html)

    The Network Usage DMO represents data that's transmitted over a network, such as a computer network or a telecommunications network. Network communications data stores details, such as source and destination IP addresses, timestamps, and data types (for example, TCP and UDP).

-   [**Operating Hours DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-operating-hours-dmo.html)

    The Operating Hours DMO represents the availability of a business or business function.

-   [**Opportunity DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-opportunity-dmo.html)

    The Opportunity DMO represents deals or sales that are in progress.

-   [**Opportunity Product DMO**](/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-opportunity-product-dmo.html)

    The Opportunity Product DMO represents a specific product or service being purchased and delivered to the client or prospect.
